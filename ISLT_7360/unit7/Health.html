Class Description:

Family and Individual Health This course will focus on the importance of family wellness. Students will experience health skills, such as decision-making, goal setting, building self-esteem, communication skills, refusal skills and conflict resolution. Topics will include personal health, mental and emotional health, relationships and sexuality, violence prevention, consumer and community health issues and safety and first aid.
