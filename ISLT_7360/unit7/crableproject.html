Hello! I am Mrs. Crable and I will be your teacher this semester! I am so excited to teach each and every one of you guys. Before we get too far let me tell you a little bit about myself. I have been married for 4 years to my husband, Josh, and we live locally with our 2 dogs, Bear and June, and 2 cats, Hawkeye and Trapper. I graduated from the University of Central Missouri in December 2010 with my Bachelor's degree in Education, with an emphasis in Family and Consumer Sciences. I am currently attending Mizzou and pursuing my Master's degree in Educational Technology. In my free time I love to spend time with my family, play with my pets, read, and do a variety of crafts. My favorite book is The Woman in Cabin 10. My favorite movie is The Notebook. My favorite music genre is country. If I were to choose a vacation destination it would be the beach.

I cannot wait to get to know everyone and I hope we all have a wonderful semester together!
