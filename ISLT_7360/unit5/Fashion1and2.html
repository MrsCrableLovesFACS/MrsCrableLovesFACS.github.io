Welcome to Fashion 1 and 2.


Course Description:

Fashion Basics I is a semester course where students are introduced to the exciting and rapidly changing field of fashion, clothing construction, and the technology that supports this field. Students will construct projects using developed sewing skills through the use of a serger, a sewing machine, and an embroidery machine. Fashion careers will also be explored. All supplies for projects are the responsibility of the student.
